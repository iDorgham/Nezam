# SEO Research — Nightclub System (Egypt Focus)

> **Market:** Egypt (Cairo, North Coast, Sharm El Sheikh)
> **Dialect:** Egyptian Arabic (Masri) + English
> **Focus:** Egyptian search habits and transliterations (Franco/Guest List).

## 1. Target Keywords & Search Intent

### 1.1 Commercial Intent (High Value)
Egyptians often use a mix of transliterated English and local Egyptian words (like "ترابيزة" for table).

| Keyword (Arabic/Masri) | Keyword (English) | Intent | Target Page |
|---|---|---|---|
| حجز ترابيزة نايت كلوب | Nightclub table booking | Transactional | Home / Booking |
| جيست ليست ديسكو | Club guest list | Transactional | Guest List |
| حجز VIP في ملهى | VIP table reservation | Transactional | VIP Booking |
| حجز سهرات الساحل | North Coast nightlife booking | Transactional | Seasonal / Events |

### 1.2 Informational Intent
| Keyword (Arabic/Masri) | Keyword (English) | Intent | Target Page |
|---|---|---|---|
| أحسن ديسكو في القاهرة | Best club in Cairo | Informational | Guide / Blog |
| أماكن سهر في الساحل | Nightlife places in Sahel | Informational | Events Page |

## 2. Cultural & Search Habits (Egypt)
- **Franco-Arabic / Arabizi:** Many users may search using Latin characters for Arabic words (e.g., "7gz trabeza"). While we won't target these in slugs, we should consider them in metadata or search fallbacks.
- **Transliteration:** Words like "جيست ليست" (Guest List) and "ديسكو" (Disco) are extremely common in the nightlife scene in Egypt.
- **Seasonality:** Massive search volume shift to the North Coast (الساحل) during summer months (June - September). The system needs to support seasonal venue highlighting.

## 3. URL Slug Rules & Structure
- Slugs remain LTR English for stability.
- Examples:
  - `/cairo/clubs/[club-name]`
  - `/sahel/events/[event-name]`
  - `/book/table/[reservation-id]`
